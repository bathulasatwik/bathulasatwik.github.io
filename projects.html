Selected Projects

Gating Enables Curvature: A Geometric Expressivity Gap in Attention
A theory-driven project studying how ungated attention induces structurally flat representation families under affine mixture geometry, while multiplicative gating enables richer curved manifolds. Focuses on curvature-based expressivity gaps and architectural inductive biases.
Sensitivity vs Sufficiency in Mechanistic Interpretability
A project examining probing, causal interventions, and representation-level evidence in interpretability workflows, with emphasis on distinguishing correlation from causal relevance in learned representations.
