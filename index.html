Welcome

I am an MS student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Southern California, specializing in Machine Learning and Data Science. My research interests lie in machine learning theory, geometric deep learning, and representation geometry in neural architectures.

My current work focuses on geometric expressivity, curvature, and architectural structure in neural networks, with particular interest in attention, multiplicative gating, and information-geometric viewpoints on learned representations.
