Current Research

A non-exhaustive overview of research directions I am currently interested in:

  • Representation Geometry
    • Study of geometric structure in learned representations
    • Structural constraints induced by architectural design
    • Intrinsic and extrinsic geometry of representation spaces
  • Architectural Expressivity
    • Comparative expressivity across neural architectures
    • Role of gating, depth, and composition in expanding function classes
    • Minimal architectural requirements for realizing complex representations
  • Depth and Structural Complexity
    • Expressivity gains induced by depth
    • Higher-order structure in deep representations
    • Interaction between depth and representation structure
  • Theoretical Limits of Learning Systems
    • Architecture-induced limits independent of optimization
    • Separation between representational capacity and trainability
    • Fundamental constraints on what learning systems can realize
    • Toward principled notions of expressivity beyond parameter count
  • Foundations and Meta-Research
    • Meaningful measures of expressivity for modern learning systems
    • Role of abstraction and toy models in understanding learning systems
    • Bridging formal theory with empirical phenomena
    • Toward mathematically grounded frameworks for modern machine learning
