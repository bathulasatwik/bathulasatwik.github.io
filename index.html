Master’s student in Electrical and Computer Engineering (MLDS) at the University of Southern California, with a B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from IIT Bhubaneswar.

My work is centered on developing mathematically principled approaches to understanding learning systems, with an emphasis on rigor, generalization, and fundamental limits. I am particularly interested in designing frameworks that ensure trustworthiness, transparency, and strong theoretical guarantees in modern machine learning.

I don’t just ask how a system can be improved, I ask what is the best it can possibly achieve, characterize those limits, and design systems that approach them.

Always happy to discuss exciting ideas and research directions — feel free to reach out.
